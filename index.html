About Us

Welcome To

From the heart of Egypt, amidst the bustling streets and timeless stories, Koshary El Tahrir began its journey in 1963 with one simple goal: to serve a truly authentic Egyptian dish with unmatched quality and unforgettable flavor. Over the years, we’ve stayed true to our original recipe and the love that made our name a household favorite. At Koshary El Tahrir, we serve koshary just the way you like it—hot, hearty, and fresh, with our signature sauces that keep you coming back for more. Every detail matters to us, from carefully selected ingredients to the way we present your meal, ensuring a dining experience that brings tradition and taste together. Welcome to Koshary El Tahrir—the flavor you grew up with, and the one we proudly continue to share.
